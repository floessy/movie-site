How to submit a movie:

  1. First You need to select a genre which the movie belongs to.

  2. After You selected the genre you need to put in the title of the movie in the "Movie Title" box.

  3. When You have putted in a movie name you need to paste the movie URL in the "movie URL" box.

  4. At last, when You are done with the movie submission, You need to click on the submit button to submit your movie to our website.
